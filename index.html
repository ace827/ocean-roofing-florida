FAQ

Common Questions

Honest answers to what homeowners most often ask us before getting started.

Do I need an appointment, or can I call anytime during business hours?
You can call us anytime during business hours at (407) 575-0022 and we'll get you on the schedule quickly. For a formal inspection and estimate, we'll set up a specific time so Joseph can give your roof the attention it deserves — we don't rush jobs or inspections.
What if I'm not sure whether I need a repair or a full replacement?
That's exactly what our inspection is for — and we'll give you an honest answer. As one of our recent customers found out, sometimes the right answer is a repair, even when the homeowner was ready to pay for a full replacement. We'll always tell you what you actually need, not what maximizes our invoice.
Do you work on all roof types in Central Florida?
Yes — we work on asphalt shingles, concrete and clay tile, metal, and flat/low-slope systems. Florida has a wide variety of roof types, and our team is experienced across all of them. If you're unsure what type of roof you have, we'll identify it during the inspection.
How quickly can you get to my property?
For standard inspections and estimates, we typically get out within a few days. For storm damage and active leaks, we prioritize rapid response — often the same day or next day — to prevent further damage to your home's interior. Call us directly at (407) 575-0022 for urgent situations.
Will you call me before starting any additional work or adding costs?
Always. We provide a written estimate before any work begins, and if something unexpected comes up during the job, we stop and call you before proceeding. You will never be surprised by your invoice — what we quote is what you pay unless you approve changes.
Can you help me file a roofing insurance claim?
Yes, and this is one of the most valuable things we do. Joseph works directly with insurance adjusters, providing detailed photo documentation and itemized damage reports that give you the best chance of a full and fair settlement. We've helped many Winter Garden homeowners navigate storm claims successfully.
Can I get a second opinion on a quote I already received?
Absolutely — we encourage it. Bring us your existing quote and we'll do a free inspection and give you our honest assessment. We won't pressure you. If the other company gave you a fair price, we'll tell you. If there are issues or a better approach, we'll walk you through it and let you decide.
